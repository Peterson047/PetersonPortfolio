HUGDOG

HUGDOG is an application that helps you to care your pet more easily. You can register your pet’s information like his/her Birthday, Sex, Breed and more. Also, you can handle your pet’s Calender, Activity, Gallery, Schedule and History. And you can easily add your other pets at HUGDOG by just click ‘Add Pet’ button. Many useful function can be used by user at slide menu. Records, Notifiactions, and Favorite menu are available at this menu. You can also share your lovely pets on other SNS.

Smart Watch

If you are Smart watch user, you can interlock HUGDOG with your Smart watch and your Smart phone. You can check notifications of HUGDOG on Smart watch. Notifications are show up as your Calender and Schedule menu which are written by you.
