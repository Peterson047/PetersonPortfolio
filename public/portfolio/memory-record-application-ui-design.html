Memoree - Minimal UI/UX design for iOS

SNS-like application, but the contents inside 'Memoree' are all private.
You can post an image with a line of quote(2 lines max on screen for title, 3 lines for paragraph) on your wall, which no one can access on it.
The only way to show your posts to others is to share your post via other SNS services.
