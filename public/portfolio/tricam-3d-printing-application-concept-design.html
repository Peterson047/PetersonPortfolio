2-Dimensional Objects

If you want to print a 2-Dimensional object, (ex. Typography, Pattern, Drawing, etc) just take a photo of the object that you want to print. You can even print an object which is drawn by you! Tricam will make a Floor Plan of the object that you take a shot by Tricam. Adjust scale and thickness of the object. You can also trim or re-colorize your object by Tricam.

3-Dimensional Objects

If you want to print a 3-Dimensional object, just record a video 360 degrees around of the object that you want to print. Then, record a Top, Front and Right view of the object. Tricam will make a Floor Plan of the object that you take a shot by Tricam. You can adjust scale of the object. You can also trim or re-colorize your object by Tricam.

Outputs

Tricam

Nowadays, 3D Printing industry is growing up day by day and the range of it's usage is growing up too. But it is not easy for normal people to use 3D printer as experts. So we invited Tricam to make people have interset in 3D printing, and make it easier to access. What you have to do is just take a picture, adjust it, and print it with 3D printer. With it's Easy-to-use and intuitive interface, beginner who wants to use 3D printer can have considerable accessibility and interest with 3D printing. Also, there is a platform that can share their Floor Plans and Projects in Tricam, so people can reference these projects, and it can give educational effects to users.

Other Functions

All you need to do is just select what you want to do. Easy-to-go and intuitive. If You made your own Floor Plan, but don’t have any 3D Printer? Don’t worry. Tricam will help you to find the nearest 3D print available design shop, based on your location. Anytime, Anywhere.
