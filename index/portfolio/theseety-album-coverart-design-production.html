Dawnstory - The Seety (Prod. Spemer)

Dawnstory is a Leader of Crew SBW(Based in Jeonju, S.Korea). This song is about Instant Love and Loneliness behind a Fance of City. Spemer(Hyouk SEO) is a member of the Crew SBW, as a position of Music Producer and Graphic Designer. He designed this Cover Art as a meaning of 'The See(Ci)+ty', and It looks like a view through a Camera. He took this Picture inside of Subway tunnel, and it's one of the Symbolic Icon of the City, But in the Full of Darkness.
