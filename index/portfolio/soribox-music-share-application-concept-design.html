SoriBox

SoriBox is an application which helps people to upload their favorite music easily. People also can share their music with another people not only on Soribox, but also other SNS like Facebook or Instagram. They can make playlists on Soribox and write a comment to other musics. It shows popular musics in Soribox based on time, and you can listen to musics which are uploaded or reposted by people who are followed by you on timeline. Uploaded musics, number of posts, followings and followers are show up on users wall. You can also sign in into Soribox not only by the account of Soribox, but also by the account of Facebook.
